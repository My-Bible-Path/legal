Privacy Policy

My Bible Path

Effective Date: February 24, 2026

My Bible Path ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Account Information. When you sign in with Apple, we receive a unique user identifier and, if you choose to share it, your name and email address. Apple may provide a private relay email address instead of your real email.

Usage Data. We collect anonymous analytics about how you interact with the app, such as journeys viewed and features used. This data does not personally identify you.

AI Tutor Conversations. When you use the AI Tutor feature, your messages are sent to a third-party AI service (OpenRouter / underlying model providers) to generate responses. We do not store conversation history on our servers beyond the current session.

Purchase Information. Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's features, including personalized reading progress
  • To process AI Tutor requests and deliver responses
  • To manage your subscription status
  • To improve the app through anonymous usage analytics

Data Storage & Security

Your reading progress and preferences are stored locally on your device and may be synced via our cloud service (Supabase) to enable cross-device access. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data in transit and at rest.

Third-Party Services

The app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Apple Sign In — for authentication
  • Supabase — for cloud data storage and authentication
  • OpenRouter — for AI Tutor chat functionality
  • RevenueCat — for subscription management

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their respective policies.

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the app's Profile settings or by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

My Bible Path is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of analytics collection

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy within the app or on this page with a revised effective date.
